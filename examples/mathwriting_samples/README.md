# MathWriting Sample Fixture

Small curated subset of real InkML samples copied from the MathWriting 2024 excerpt for the example notebook.

These samples are intentionally full expressions rather than standalone symbols, so the notebook renders meaningful formula pages out of the box. The fixture includes equations, fractions, integrals, sums, and matrix/array expressions.

The samples are derived from MathWriting and follow the MathWriting dataset license terms.
